Context Portability

Carry your conversation across Claude, ChatGPT, and Gemini without losing context

How it works

When you finish a conversation, TokenWise compresses it into a 150–300 token summary. Switch to any other AI model and that context is injected automatically — your follow-up makes sense even though the model never saw your original conversation.

Why this is unique

No AI platform will build this — Claude has no incentive to carry GPT context, and vice versa. Only a neutral tool like TokenWise can work across all models simultaneously.
