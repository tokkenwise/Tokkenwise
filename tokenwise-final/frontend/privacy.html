Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026
The short version: We collect only what's needed to run TokenWise. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. Your prompts are yours.

1. Who We Are

TokenWise is operated by Ussama Bin Tariq ("we", "us", "our"). We provide a token counting and AI cost tracking tool available at this website and as a Chrome extension.

2. What We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address and name when you create an account
  • Payment information processed securely by LemonSqueezy (we never store card details)

Information collected automatically when you use TokenWise:

  • Token counts and word counts from prompts you analyze
  • Which AI model you were using
  • Which website the analysis was performed on (e.g. chat.openai.com)
  • Estimated cost calculations
  • Timestamp of each session

What we do NOT collect:

  • The actual text content of your prompts (unless you use the compression feature)
  • Your conversations with AI tools
  • Browsing history outside of AI tool pages

3. Prompt Compression Feature

When you use the "Analyze & Compress" feature, your prompt text is sent to our secure server and then to Anthropic's API for processing. We store the original and compressed versions to improve our service and to show you your compression history. You can delete this data at any time from Settings → Delete all my data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the TokenWise service and your usage dashboard
  • To send budget alert emails when you set a threshold
  • To send account-related emails (confirmation, password reset)
  • To improve the accuracy of our token counting and cost estimates
  • To process payments through LemonSqueezy

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database) hosted on AWS in the Asia-Pacific region. We use Row Level Security (RLS) to ensure users can only access their own data. All data is encrypted in transit using TLS.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider
  • Netlify — hosting provider
  • LemonSqueezy — payment processor (receives your email and payment details)
  • Anthropic — receives prompt text only when you use the compression feature
  • Resend — email delivery service (receives your email address to send emails)
  • Law enforcement — if required by law

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all data we hold about you — email us
  • Delete your data — Settings → Delete all my data, or email us
  • Export your data — Dashboard → Export CSV
  • Correct inaccurate data — Settings → Account
  • Object to processing — email us and we will stop

8. Cookies

TokenWise uses only essential cookies required for authentication (session tokens). We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

TokenWise is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use after changes means you accept the new policy.
