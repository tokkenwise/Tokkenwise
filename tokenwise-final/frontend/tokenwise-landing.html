FAQ
Common questions
Does it read my prompts? +
TokenWise counts tokens locally in your browser. Your prompt text never leaves your device for counting. The AI compression feature (Pro) sends your prompt to our server only when you explicitly click "Analyze" — you're always in control.
Which AI tools does it work on? +
Free plan: ChatGPT, Claude.ai, Gemini. Pro plan: all of the above plus Notion AI, Perplexity, Gmail, LinkedIn, and any custom site with a text input (via our universal mode).
How accurate are the token counts? +
Very accurate — within 1-3 tokens of the actual API count in most cases. We use a client-side tokenizer that closely mirrors each model's actual tokenization algorithm.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes, absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period. No questions, no friction.

Yes — 7 days free, no credit card required. You get full Pro access during the trial. We'll remind you 2 days before it ends.
